# Solo Leveling System - Complete Reference Guide

## üìã Core System Features from Anime/Manga

### 1. **Status Window / Stat System**
- **Attributes Displayed:**
  - Strength
  - Agility
  - Stamina/Vitality
  - Intelligence
  - Sense
- **Stat Allocation:** Points received on level-up can be manually allocated
- **Visual:** Blue holographic interface
- **Real-time Updates:** Stats change immediately when allocated

### 2. **Quest System**
- **Daily Quests:** Regular tasks that reset daily
  - Must be completed or face penalties
  - Rewards: EXP, items, stat points
- **Weekly Quests:** Longer-term objectives
- **Hidden Quests:** Discovered through specific actions
- **Emergency Quests:** Urgent missions with time limits
- **Penalty Quests:** Mandatory tasks with consequences for failure
- **Quest Types:**
  - Survival quests
  - Training quests
  - Combat quests
  - Collection quests

### 3. **Leveling System**
- **Unlimited Growth:** Unlike other hunters, can level up infinitely
- **EXP Sources:**
  - Completing quests
  - Defeating monsters
  - Completing dungeons
- **Level-up Rewards:**
  - Stat points to allocate
  - Skill points
  - New abilities unlocked
  - Access to higher-tier content

### 4. **Skill System**
- **Skill Acquisition:**
  - Rewarded from quests
  - Purchased from shop
  - Learned through skill books
  - Unlocked at certain levels
- **Skill Types:**
  - Active Skills (Stealth, Dash, etc.)
  - Passive Skills (auto-activate when needed)
  - Combat Skills
  - Utility Skills
- **Skill Enhancement:** Can upgrade existing skills
- **Skill Combination:** Can combine skills for more powerful effects

### 5. **Inventory System**
- **Unlimited Storage:** No physical limitations
- **Item Categories:**
  - Weapons
  - Armor
  - Consumables (potions, elixirs)
  - Materials
  - Skill Books
  - Rare Items (Elixir of Life, etc.)
- **Item Management:** Easy access and organization
- **Item Details:** Shows stats, effects, descriptions

### 6. **Shop System**
- **Currency:** Gold earned from quests and monster drops
- **Available Items:**
  - Weapons
  - Armor
  - Consumables
  - Skill Books
  - Equipment
- **Item Rarity:** Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary
- **Dynamic Pricing:** Based on item value

### 7. **Instant Dungeons**
- **Private Dungeons:** Created by the System
- **Features:**
  - Tailored to current level
  - Controlled environment
  - No external interference
  - Training opportunities
  - Resource gathering
  - Quest completion
- **Access:** Can be entered anytime

### 8. **Titles & Achievements**
- **Titles:** Awarded for accomplishments
- **Title Effects:**
  - Provide stat bonuses
  - Unlock special abilities
  - Grant passive effects
- **Examples:**
  - "The Strongest Hunter"
  - "Dungeon Conqueror"
  - "Monster Slayer"

### 9. **Job/Class System**
- **Job Changes:** Major evolution points
- **Shadow Monarch:** Ultimate class evolution
- **Job Benefits:**
  - New abilities
  - Stat multipliers
  - Unique skills
  - Special powers

### 10. **Alert & Notification System**
- **Real-time Alerts:**
  - Quest updates
  - Level up notifications
  - Threat detection (murderous intent)
  - Item acquisition
  - Skill unlocks
- **Visual Style:** Blue holographic popups
- **Non-intrusive:** Appears when needed
- **System Messages:** Authoritative, game-like tone

### 11. **Life Protection Mechanism**
- **Auto-activation:** Passive skills activate automatically when needed
- **Threat Detection:** Warns of imminent dangers
- **Survival Focus:** System actively works to keep player alive

### 12. **Reward System**
- **Quest Rewards:**
  - Experience Points (EXP)
  - Stat Points
  - Items
  - Skill Books
  - Gold
  - Rare Materials
- **Reward Screen:** "QUEST REWARDS" popup
- **Accept/Decline:** Can accept or decline rewards
- **Random Loot Boxes:** Sometimes included

### 13. **Penalty System**
- **Quest Failure:** Consequences for not completing mandatory quests
- **Daily Quest Penalties:** Must complete daily quests
- **Warning Messages:** System warns about penalties

### 14. **UI Design Elements**
- **Color Scheme:**
  - Primary: Blue holographic
  - Accent: Purple (in some versions)
  - Gold/Black (alternative theme)
- **Visual Effects:**
  - Glowing borders
  - Holographic effects
  - Scan lines
  - Circuit board patterns
- **Typography:**
  - Futuristic fonts
  - Bold, authoritative text
  - Clear hierarchy

### 15. **System Communication**
- **Tone:** Authoritative, game-like
- **Format:** "[System] Message"
- **Language:** Direct, clear instructions
- **Personality:** Minimal, focused on functionality
